Projects

TIE Fighter Defense

Tie Fighter Defense is a Star Wars themed game. The point of the game is to shoot the TIE fighter before it reaches the bottom of the board. If the player shoots the TIE Fighter before it reaches the end of the board, it will re-appear at the top of the board, in a random position, moving at a faster speed. The point of the game is to see how many points you can get. Have fun!

Technologies used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery.

produswap.org

produswap.org connects people with surplus home grown produce that they can swap with each other. This helps families save time and money and prevents great produce from going to waste.

Technologies used: Ruby on Rails, HTML5, SCSS, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL, BCrypt, Heroku, PowerPoint, GoDaddy, Photoshop Elements 11, Canon T3i.

stockclosr.com

stockclosr.com allows users to create a stock watch list using a third party API. Users can also attempt to predict the closing price of a stock. The user’s prediction along with the community’s aggregated and averaged prediction is displayed on the watch list.

Framework: Javscript: Node.js, Express, jQuery, npm Database: MongoDB, RoboMongo (data vizualization) Front End: HTML5, CCS3 + Sass, template from 'HTML5 Up'

MARIGOLD.Life

If a loved one passed away, would you know who to call? Do you know how much it would cost? MARIGOLD.Life assists families on one of the most difficult days of their lives by providing information on funeral providers in your city. Framework: Node.js and Express Database: MongoDB, RoboMongo (data vizualization) Front End: HTML5, CCS3, Angular, Angular Material Design
